REVIEW

Nelson Decamps

April 21, 2026

Tron: Ares introduces a futuristic concept where a powerful digital program enters the real world, creating a conflict between humans and technology. The movie explores what can happen when technology grows faster than our ability to control it, and how dangerous it can become if it is not used responsibly. It also reflects how focusing too much on power, control, and advanced systems instead of human values can lead to serious consequences.

The movie also puts into perspective how cybersecurity could look if situations like this became real. It shows how digital systems, if not properly secured, could become a major threat when they interact with the real world. This makes you think about how important it is to protect systems, data, and infrastructure as technology continues to grow and evolve.

The visuals are very modern and give the movie a strong futuristic feel. The digital world and real world blend together in a way that makes the story more intense and engaging. The action scenes and technology shown throughout the movie keep the viewer interested and make it feel fast-paced.

Overall, Tron: Ares is a strong sci-fi movie that shows both the potential and the risks of advanced technology. It is not just about action, but also about responsibility, control, cybersecurity, and the future of how humans interact with machines.
